Refurbished inventory has a high amount of repeat data entry, but the useful details still come from a technician who has the machine in front of them.

The goal is not to remove the human check. The goal is to make the repetitive parts faster: serial capture, model lookup, condition templates, photo naming, product drafts, and stock handoff.

## Intake flow

1. Record the device identity.
2. Run health checks.
3. Grade condition with a consistent rubric.
4. Generate a product draft from approved fields.
5. Hold for a final human review before publish.
